Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

We accept most major dental insurance plans including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, United Healthcare, and many more. Our front desk team will verify your benefits before your appointment so there are no surprises.

Patient comfort is our top priority. We use advanced numbing techniques, the finest anesthetics, and offer sedation options for anxious patients. Most patients are surprised by how comfortable their treatment is — even procedures they feared most.

We typically offer next-day appointments for new patients, and same-day slots are reserved for dental emergencies. Book online or call us at (555) 123-4567 and we'll find the earliest available time that works for your schedule.

We offer 0% interest financing through CareCredit for up to 24 months on qualifying treatments. We also offer in-house payment plans and will work with your budget to make sure you can access the care you need without financial stress.

Yes! We love treating the whole family. The American Dental Association recommends a child's first dental visit by age one or when the first tooth appears. Our pediatric dentist Dr. Sharma specializes in making children feel safe and excited about dental care.

Most patients benefit from checkups and cleanings every 6 months. However, patients with gum disease, a history of cavities, or other risk factors may need to come in every 3-4 months. We'll create a personalized schedule for you at your first visit.
